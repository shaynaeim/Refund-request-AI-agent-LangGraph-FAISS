# Standard Operating Procedure: Return Eligibility Timeframes

**Document ID:** SOP-RET-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 2024  
**Review Date:** January 2025  

## 1. PURPOSE

This document establishes the standard timeframes for return eligibility across all product categories to ensure consistent customer service and operational efficiency.

## 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all products sold through our e-commerce platform and covers return timeframes from the date of delivery.

## 3. RETURN WINDOW PERIODS

### 3.1 Electronics Category
- **Standard Electronics:** 14-30 days from delivery date
- **High-Value Electronics (>$1000):** 30 days from delivery date
- **Gaming Equipment:** 14-30 days from delivery date
- **Cameras & Photography:** 14-30 days from delivery date
- **Tablets & Laptops:** 14-30 days from delivery date
- **Smartphones:** 14-30 days from delivery date
- **Headphones:** 14-30 days from delivery date

**Special Conditions for Electronics:**
- Product must be in original packaging with all accessories
- No physical damage beyond normal handling
- Software/digital content downloads void return eligibility
- Activation of cellular devices may void return eligibility

### 3.2 Clothing & Accessories
- **Clothing (All Categories):** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Shoes:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Accessories:** 14-90 days from delivery date
- **Activewear:** 30-90 days from delivery date

**Special Conditions for Clothing:**
- Items must have original tags attached
- No signs of wear, washing, or alteration
- Undergarments and swimwear: final sale (no returns)
- Special occasion items: 30-day maximum window

### 3.3 Home & Garden
- **Furniture:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Kitchen Items:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Bedding:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Home Decor:** 14-90 days from delivery date
- **Tools:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Garden Equipment:** 45-90 days from delivery date

**Special Conditions for Home & Garden:**
- Furniture assembly voids return eligibility unless defective
- Kitchen appliances must be unused and in original packaging
- Plants and perishable garden items: 14-day maximum

### 3.4 Books
- **All Book Categories:** 14-90 days from delivery date
- **Digital Books/E-books:** No returns after download
- **Educational Materials:** 30-90 days from delivery date

**Special Conditions for Books:**
- Physical books must be in resalable condition
- No writing, highlighting, or damage to pages
- Opened software packages included with books void return eligibility

### 3.5 Sports & Fitness
- **Fitness Equipment:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Outdoor Gear:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Team Sports Equipment:** 14-90 days from delivery date
- **Water Sports:** 30-90 days from delivery date
- **Winter Sports:** 14-90 days from delivery date
- **Cycling Equipment:** 30-90 days from delivery date

**Special Conditions for Sports:**
- Equipment showing signs of use beyond testing voids return eligibility
- Safety equipment (helmets, protective gear): no returns if used
- Seasonal items have reduced return windows during peak seasons

## 4. RETURN WINDOW CALCULATION

### 4.1 Start Date
The return window begins counting from the **delivery date** as confirmed by:
- Carrier delivery confirmation
- Customer delivery confirmation
- GPS tracking confirmation
- Signature confirmation

### 4.2 End Date Calculation
- Return window includes weekends and holidays
- Return requests must be initiated before 11:59 PM on the final eligible day
- Timezone based on customer's shipping address

### 4.3 Grace Period
- **Standard Grace Period:** 2 business days beyond stated window for processing delays
- **Holiday Grace Period:** 5 business days extension during major holiday periods (November-January)
- **Weather/Natural Disaster Grace Period:** Case-by-case extension up to 14 days

## 5. EXCEPTIONS TO STANDARD TIMEFRAMES

### 5.1 Defective Products
- No time limit for manufacturing defects
- Warranty period takes precedence over return window
- Must provide proof of defect (photos, videos, documentation)

### 5.2 Wrong Item Shipped
- 90-day window regardless of product category
- Must provide evidence of shipping error
- Original item must be returned for replacement

### 5.3 Damaged During Shipping
- 30-day window from delivery date
- Must report damage within 48 hours of delivery
- Requires photos of packaging and product damage

### 5.4 Gift Returns
- Extended window: 90 days from original delivery date OR 30 days from gift recipient notification
- Gift receipt or order confirmation required
- Subject to same condition requirements as regular returns

## 6. VALIDATION PROCEDURES

### 6.1 Return Request Validation
1. Verify delivery date through order management system
2. Calculate elapsed days from delivery to request date
3. Confirm product category and applicable return window
4. Check for applicable grace periods or exceptions
5. Validate customer tier for potential extensions

### 6.2 Documentation Requirements
- Order number or receipt
- Reason for return
- Photos if claiming damage or defect
- Original packaging for electronics

## 7. ESCALATION PROCEDURES

### 7.1 Out-of-Window Requests
- Review customer history and tier status
- Evaluate reason for delay in return request
- Consider one-time courtesy exceptions for loyal customers
- Document any approved exceptions with justification

### 7.2 Dispute Resolution
- Customer service manager approval required for exceptions beyond grace period
- Regional manager approval for exceptions beyond 30 days of window
- All exceptions must be documented with business justification

## 8. MONITORING AND COMPLIANCE

### 8.1 Performance Metrics
- Track return request approval rates by product category
- Monitor grace period utilization
- Analyze exception patterns for policy improvements

### 8.2 Policy Review
- Quarterly review of return patterns and timeframes
- Annual policy update based on business needs and customer feedback
- Seasonal adjustments for holiday periods
